Bio

Aaron Ruddick writes songs that live between memory, confession, cinema, and a quiet kind of magic. Each release aims to make the emotion feel real, give the music a visual world, and invite the listener inside. Drawn to fantasy, mystery, love, and the strange beauty of ordinary moments, he uses music as a place to live between worlds and express feelings through magical landscapes.
