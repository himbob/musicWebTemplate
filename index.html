Bio

Aaron Ruddick writes songs that live between memory, confession, cinema, and a quiet kind of magic. His sound shifts with each story, but the goal stays the same: make the emotion feel real, give it a visual world, and let the song invite the listener inside. Drawn to fantasy, mystery, love, and the strange beauty of ordinary moments, he uses music as a place to live between worlds and express feelings through magical landscapes.
