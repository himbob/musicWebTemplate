About

Hi, I'm Aaron Ruddick, a singer-songwriter based in California. I blend heartfelt stories with soulful melodies to create music that connects with listeners. Welcome to my official site!

  • Read Bio

  • Aaron is a California-based singer-songwriter and a student at Berklee School of Music. Born in Nebraska, he is a veteran of the first Gulf War. After spending much of his life as a software engineer, Aaron is now fully dedicating himself back to his true passion—music.

    Rejecting the idea of sticking to a single genre, Aaron creates songs that allow the story and emotion to take center stage. His work spans a wide range of styles, from heartfelt romantic ballads to witty and playful comedy tracks, reflecting his belief that music should transcend boundaries.

    Many of Aaron's current projects, written during his studies at Berklee, explore diverse topics and genres, showcasing his versatility and creativity as a songwriter. Through his music, Aaron strives to connect deeply with listeners and share his stories in ways that resonate and inspire.
