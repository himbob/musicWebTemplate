Artist biography

Short bio

Aaron Ruddick is a singer-songwriter and music producer based in Santa Cruz, California. His music blends soulful storytelling, cinematic atmosphere, and indie-pop sensibility, often focusing on longing, memory, and the search for something meaningful inside everyday life.

His upcoming single, Stir My Cup in Time, turns a simple act into a supernatural ritual. Built around a warm, intimate vocal and a shifting Latin-inspired pulse, the song follows a man stirring his coffee in hopes of briefly bringing back the presence of someone deeply loved.

Long bio

Aaron Ruddick is a singer-songwriter and music producer based in Santa Cruz, California. His work lives at the intersection of soulful storytelling, cinematic mood, and indie-pop songwriting. Across his music, he returns to themes of longing, memory, romance, and the quiet hope that ordinary moments might still hold something mysterious.

His upcoming single, Stir My Cup in Time, captures that instinct directly. The song centers on a man who stirs a cup of coffee as if performing a ritual, hoping to summon the fleeting return of someone he deeply loves. Guided by an intimate vocal, vivid imagery, and a subtle Latin-inspired rhythmic movement, the track blends warmth, melancholy, and a touch of magic.

Before fully committing to music, Ruddick spent years in programming and engineering. That background still informs the care and precision in his work, but songwriting and production have become the place where he speaks most honestly. As a father, veteran, and Berklee student, he brings lived experience to songs that aim to feel personal, vivid, and emotionally real.
