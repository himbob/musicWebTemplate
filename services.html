Professional Services

Production & Songwriting

Listening-first services that help songs move from rough ideas to finished, shareable releases.

Song Development & Co-Writing

Turning ideas, stories, and emotions into finished songs centered around your voice and intent. Lyric shaping, groove development, melody exploration, and structure support as needed.

Arrangement & Groove Shaping

Simplifying clutter and shaping movement so the song feels grounded and alive. Focus on groove, dynamics, and making space for what matters most.

Vocal Editing & Alignment

Comping, timing, tuning, and cleanup that keeps performances human and emotionally intact. Alignment that locks to the groove without sounding robotic.

Mix Finishing

Balancing stems, cleaning up low end and mids, and bringing clarity and cohesion so your song feels ready to be shared professionally.

Independent Release Mastering

Preparing songs for independent release by shaping tone, balance, and loudness to match a reference track. Ideal for artists who want their music to sound cohesive and enjoyable for personal releases, streaming, or sharing.

Stem Splitting & Recovery

Separating vocals and instruments from a stereo track to regain control for mixing, arrangement changes, or remixing. Results depend on the source material, but useful when original sessions are unavailable.

Song Feedback & Clarity Sessions

Focused listening and guidance to help identify what is working, what is fighting the song, and where to focus next. Ideal when you are stuck and want a clear plan for the next revision.
